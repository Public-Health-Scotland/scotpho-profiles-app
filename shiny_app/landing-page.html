What's new

Working together with the Care and Wellbeing Portfolio

ScotPHO will be working with Scottish Government and other colleagues on future development of the Care and Wellbeing Portfolio Dashboard to assist with their ambition of providing data and intelligence to improve population health, address health inequalities and improve the health and care system.

Profile summary design

Our ‘Profiles Summary’ page has been re-designed bringing back the spine chart style visualisations.

Inequalities indicators

We have expanded the range of indicators available within the ‘Inequalities’ page, these now include indicators where an increasing value indicates a more favourable outcome such as life expectancy.
